Background

A few historical steps in the company's development

  • 1962

    Machine Tools

    1962 Mister Claude BENAY becomes head of CEDEMI S.A. The company focuses on new and second hand chip-forming machine tools.

  • 1970-1971

    Company's headquarters relocate to Villeneuve la Garenne

    1970 The company specialises in very large machines.

    1971 Head office creation and relocation to Villeneuve la Garenne, in order to handle "heavy duty" machines.

  • 1972

    Buyouts

    Takeover of the Facon & Cie company in the "port autonome de Paris" (Gennevilliers) to increase our storage surface. Collaboration with HLMO manufacturer.

  • 1995

    Export

    1995 Company restructuring, export market development.

    2005 CEDEMI leaves its premises in the Gennevilliers port.
